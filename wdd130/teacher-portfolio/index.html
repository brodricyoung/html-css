Professional Teacher Portfolio

Introduction

Welcome to my professional teacher portfolio. In these digital pages, I invite you to explore seven learning and instructional standards for educators set by the International Society for Technology in Education (ISTE). These standards have provided a framework for innovative lesson planning, assessments, and collaborative work and they empower me with the knowledge I need to thrive as an educator in a technology-driven world. Throughout the pages, you will find a collection of artifacts, reflections, and evidence of this framework. This portfolio reflects my growth and commitment to the ISTE standards and to creating a nurturing environment for my students that fosters a sense of curiosity and a lifelong love for learning.

Teaching philosophy

Before I learned to read, I loved listening to my mom read stacks, very large stacks, of books to me every day. This daily ritual not only instilled in me a deep love for stories but also laid the foundation for my educational journey. Story time, as a cherished and formative experience, not only prepared me for the academic challenges of school but also equipped me with essential life skills.

As an educator, I recognize the enduring value of storytelling and its pivotal role in fostering a love for learning. While my personal connection to story time remains steadfast, I acknowledge that today's learners are navigating a world enriched with a variety of technologies. In this fast-paced, technology-driven era, my philosophy centers on harnessing these tools to enhance instruction and maximize the potential of every student.

Understanding that my attitude, actions, and teaching approach significantly influence the learning environment, I embrace the role of an innovative educator. In a landscape marked by rapid growth and constant change, I position myself as a perpetual learner, dedicated to continuous improvement. I view technology not as a substitute for traditional methods but as a powerful tool that, when used effectively, can amplify the learning experience. By weaving together the timeless magic of storytelling with the possibilities afforded by technology, I aim to cultivate not only knowledgeable individuals but lifelong learners who find joy and purpose in their educational journey.
